About Us

The project, LearnIT2SingIT, is a music score sheet web application that houses a large database of music sheets and other music resources. The music sheet will be for music enthusiasts interested in using the resources for private practice or choral groups. This will make learning easy, interesting, and at the click of a button. This dynamic website will allow users to search for music sheets and preview them. Registered members will have access to an unlimited number of songs to download while unregistered members will be restricted to a limited number.
